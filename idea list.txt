General Rules
1) When a player kills someone, the person they killed joins their team.
2) When a team has no players, they lose.
3) If 'Gamemode Settings - Mode' #1 is enabled, other teams can only win if other Victory Methods are enabled.

Gamemode Settings - Mode
1) Only team 1 can switch players [bool]
2) Amount of deaths until player switches team [1-10]
3) On friendly kill, player switches to random opposing team [bool]
4) End round on first team elimated [bool]

Gamemode Settings - Player
1) Switch Grace Period (time you have before a death by an opposing team switches you)

Gamemode Settings - Respawn
1) Switched Team Penalty [0-inf]

Gamemode Settings - Points
1) Switched a Player
